About Us

About B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology, Pilani

B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology (BKBIET), Pilani, established in 2007, is one of the premier institutes in India for technical education. With a commitment to academic excellence, BKBIET aims to produce competent professionals with a strong ethical foundation. The institute is known for its excellent academic curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and focus on holistic development.

Director

Dr. Ramesh Chandra

Director, B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology

Dr. Ramesh Chandra has over 30 years of experience in education and administration. Under his leadership, the institute has achieved numerous milestones, enhancing academic excellence and research output.

Principal

Prof. Sunita Sharma

Principal, B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology

Prof. Sunita Sharma is dedicated to promoting quality education and fostering an environment conducive to learning and research. Her vision is to prepare students for global challenges in the field of technology and innovation.

Founder

Shri B.K. Birla

Founder, B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology

Shri B.K. Birla, a visionary industrialist and philanthropist, founded the institute with a vision to offer affordable, world-class technical education. His legacy continues to inspire the institution's growth and development.

Facilities

Library

The institute boasts a well-equipped library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support academic learning and research.

Hostel

The campus provides comfortable and secure hostel accommodation for students, ensuring a safe and nurturing environment for learning.

Labs

Modern laboratories equipped with the latest equipment to provide hands-on learning experiences for students across different branches of engineering.

Placements

Many renowned companies visit the campus for placements, offering students lucrative career opportunities. Some of the top recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Capgemini, and other multinational companies.

Courses & Degrees

  • B.Tech in various engineering disciplines
  • B.Com in Commerce and Business Administration
  • Other diploma and certification programs

Exams

Students are assessed through internal assessments, mid-semester, and end-semester exams. The institute follows a rigorous evaluation process to ensure academic excellence and knowledge retention.

Sports

The institute promotes physical fitness and teamwork through various sports facilities, including cricket, basketball, football, and a fully equipped gymnasium.

Programs & Events

BKBIET organizes a range of events and programs throughout the year, such as Qusar, Basant, and Freshers' Party, fostering student engagement and community spirit.
