Yaduvanshi Shiksha Niketan
MLD Yaduvanshi Shiksha Niketan is an English Medium Co-educational Residential cum Day Boarding School up to Senior Secondary standard affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (Govt. of India) New Delhi. It is situated in the south of the village Sohali, Distt. Jhunjhunu(Rajasthan).

The school is at a distance of 16 km from Mahendergarh and 25 km from Narnaul, Haryana. The school operates on progressive modern lines while providing the facilities of modern education; it retains the best traditions of our rich Indian legacy. Library
The school has a rich library where books ranging from all-time classics to contemporary blue-ribbon books, ample reference books and support materials for board and competitive exams (i.e., NDA, JEE, NEET, CLAT, etc.) are available for students. In order to keep the students abreast of the happenings around the world, the library has all the national dailies.

Sports
Playground
True meaning of education lies in all-round development of the students viz mental, social, intellectual, and also physical development. The school has sprawling playgrounds with a 200-meter 8-lane track, fields, and courts for all outdoor games, besides an indoor stadium and a Hi-Fi Gymnasium and a well-equipped sports room. Games, a compulsory activity, ultimately aim to foster brotherhood and sportsmanship among the children.
